About Game

Instructions

  1. Click "Let's Go!" to begin. For more details, click "About the Game".
  2. Choose your level: JSS or SSS.
  3. Pick your subject and year for past exam questions.
  4. Answer 60 multipl-choice questions per round within 60 seconds each.
  5. You can skip questions and return to them later within the 1-hour playtime.
  6. Unanswered questions will appear at the end of the round if time allows.
  7. If you don't answer the question in 60 seconds, the game moves to the next question.
  8. Final scores and correct answers will be displayed at the end.
  9. Monitor the timer on the right side of the screen to manage your time.

Game Rules

  1. You must complete all questions within 1 hour
  2. You can only submit one answer per question
  3. You may skip questions and return to them within the 1-hour playtime
  4. Any unanswered questions will be presented as the end of the round is there is remaining time
  5. Each question must be answered within 60 seconds. If time runs out, the game will proceed to the next question
  6. At the end of the playtime, scores and correct answeres will be displayed
